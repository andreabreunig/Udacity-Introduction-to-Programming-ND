Shetland Sheepdog (Sheltie)

Resembling to a miniture version of Lassie, the Sheltie is a very intelligent, energetic, and loyal family dog.

  • Place of origin: Shetland Islands
  • Coat colors: Sable, tricolor, blue merle
  • Height: 33-41 cm
  • Average Lifespan: 14 years

Shelties are herding dogs that developed in the harsh environment of the Shetland Islands. Although they look like small Collies, they are not direct descendants of that breed. They love to be active, while being extremely intelligent and devoted to the owners.
